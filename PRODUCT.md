register: both — `/` and marketing surfaces are brand; `/dashboard`, `/onboarding` and everything behind them are product.

## Product purpose

BravohAi is an AI trading copilot: upload a chart screenshot (XAUUSD/Gold first, forex/crypto/indices supported) and get a professional trade plan in seconds — trend, key levels, bullish/bearish scenarios with probabilities, and entry/stop/target adapted to the user's trading profile. Modeled on bullgpt.io's product mechanics.

## Users

Retail traders, mostly beginner-to-intermediate, many on prop-firm challenges. They arrive from WhatsApp/Instagram/YouTube links, often on mobile. They want a confident answer ("what do I do on this chart?") without a TradingView learning curve. Gold traders are the core audience.

## Anti-references

- Gold-on-black "crypto bro" trading sites (the category reflex)
- Neon-glow gradient SaaS templates
- Hype copy that promises returns; we say "educational, not financial advice" plainly

## Strategic principles

- XAUUSD first: gold is the hero instrument everywhere an instrument appears.
- The no-trade call is a feature: the product refuses weak setups.
- Every analysis is saved; win-rate honesty over hype.
